About Us

The 'Watch it outside - Boston Edition' is an outdoor film festival organized by the 'Watch it outside events Co.' in Boston Public Garden from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight.

Our main purpose is to select and to show films that can appeal to audiences of all ages, that's why shows are free and open to everyone. But in order to do that, you need to pre-register so that we're able to estimate how many people might come and so that we can close registration if it reaches the maximum venueâ€™s capacity of 1,500 people.

We can't wait to help you discover awesome films!
